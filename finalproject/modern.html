The Deck

Deck Size

60 cards minimum. Unlimited deck size as long as you can shuffle it.

Card Sets Allowed

All Core, Expansions 8th Edition to Current.

Duplicates

No more than 4 copies including cards in sideboard.Excluding Basic Lands.

Sideboard

Up to 15 cards outside of the game the player may rotate into their deck between matches.

The Game

Players

2

Starting Life

20

Win Conditions

Player's life reaches 0 they lose.

Player's deck reaches 0 cards they lose.
